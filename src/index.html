About Me

Michael Ricciarelli

I was born on April 26th, 1941 in Quincy, Massachusetts. Which is where I lived until moving to Hingham, Massachusetts in 1973 with my loving family. I have a wife named Francis and three children, Craig, Michael, and Debra. I began painting when my children were young and it has been a hobby of mine ever since. I love to paint the sceneries of Italy and other places I have traveled as well as boats, flowers and people dancing. Soon my work will be available for purchase on Etsy.
